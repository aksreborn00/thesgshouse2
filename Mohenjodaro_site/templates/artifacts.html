Artifacts of Mohenjo-daro

Dancing Girl

This bronze statue, around 4,500 years old, represents a young girl in a pose suggesting dance. It's an iconic example of Indus Valley metallurgy and artistic expression.

Priest King

This carved figure, believed to be a high-ranking individual or spiritual leader, is made from steatite and showcases intricate detailing and craftsmanship.

Indus Seal

Seals were used for trade and communication. This one depicts an animal figure and Indus script, providing insight into the civilization's language and beliefs.
